💬 Got Questions?

Frequently Asked

Everything you need to know about sending gifts across borders.

How long does delivery take? ⌄
Same-day delivery is available in Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane, and Perth for orders placed before 2:00 PM. For other cities, express delivery typically takes 2–6 hours. Scheduled deliveries can be booked up to 30 days in advance.
Are the flowers guaranteed to be fresh? ⌄
Absolutely. Every bouquet is hand-arranged fresh on the day of dispatch. We offer a 100% freshness guarantee — if your recipient receives wilted flowers, we'll send a full replacement or issue a refund immediately.
Can I keep my identity as a surprise? ⌄
Yes! Our "Surprise Delivery" option lets you remain anonymous. The recipient receives the gift without any indication of who sent it — unless you choose to include a card with your name.
What cities in Australia do you deliver to? ⌄
We currently deliver to Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane, Perth, Adelaide, Canberra, Hobart, Darwin, Gold Coast, Sunshine Coast, Newcastle, Wollongong, Geelong, Townsville, and Cairns. More cities coming soon!
How do I track my order? ⌄
After placing your order, you'll receive a unique tracking ID via SMS and email. Visit our Track Order page and enter your order number to see real-time status updates, including a live map view of your delivery driver.
What payment methods do you accept? ⌄
We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. All transactions are SSL-secured and processed by Stripe.

Still have questions? Our team is available 7 days a week.
